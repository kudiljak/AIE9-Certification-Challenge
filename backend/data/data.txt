Services, Duration, and Descriptions

Haircuts

Women’s Haircut — 45 minutes
Includes consultation, hair wash, precision cutting, and basic styling. Suitable for maintaining shape, refreshing layers, or a complete style change.

Men’s Haircut — 30 minutes
A tailored haircut including consultation, clipper and scissor work, and light styling. Ideal for classic cuts, fades, and modern styles.

Children’s Haircut (under 12) — 25 minutes
A gentle and efficient haircut designed to keep children comfortable. Includes basic shaping and optional light styling.

⸻

Coloring

Root Touch-Up — 60 minutes
Covers regrowth at the roots to match the existing hair color. Recommended every 4–6 weeks to maintain a consistent look.

Full Hair Coloring — 90 minutes
Applies a single color from roots to ends for a complete color refresh or change. Includes consultation to select the best shade for your skin tone.

Highlights — 120 minutes
Adds lighter strands throughout the hair to create dimension and brightness. Can be customized for subtle or bold contrast.

Balayage — 150 minutes
A hand-painted highlighting technique that creates a natural, sun-kissed effect with soft transitions. Low-maintenance and ideal for a modern look.

⸻

Styling

Blow-Dry — 30 minutes
Hair is washed and styled using professional blow-drying techniques to create volume, smoothness, or soft waves.

Wash & Style — 40 minutes
Includes hair washing followed by styling with brushes and heat tools for a polished everyday look.

Formal Updo — 60 minutes
An elegant hairstyle suitable for special events such as weddings, galas, and celebrations. Includes consultation and secure styling.

Bridal Hairstyling — 90 minutes
A customized hairstyle for brides, including trial consultation (if pre-booked), detailed styling, and long-lasting finishing techniques.

⸻

Treatments

Deep Conditioning Treatment — 30 minutes
Restores moisture and improves hair softness using intensive nourishing products. Recommended for dry or damaged hair.

Keratin Treatment — 120 minutes
A smoothing treatment that reduces frizz, enhances shine, and makes hair more manageable for up to several months.

Scalp Treatment — 20 minutes
A targeted treatment designed to soothe irritation, reduce dandruff, and promote a healthy scalp environment.

Upsell prices apply only when the add-on is booked in the same visit as a qualifying service. The assistant can offer these when confirming a booking.
These bundle deals must be booked as a single appointment. Total duration is summed duration of single services making the bundle.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Appointments & Booking

Do I need an appointment?  
Yes, appointments are recommended for all services to ensure availability. Walk-ins are accepted only if time allows.

How can I book an appointment?  
You can book by phone, email, or through our AI booking assistant. Please provide your name, phone number, desired service, and preferred date and time.

Can I book on the same day?  
Yes, same-day bookings are possible if time slots are available.

How far in advance should I book?  
We recommend booking at least 2–3 days in advance for standard services and at least one week in advance for bridal or balayage services.

---

### Changes & Cancellations

Can I reschedule my appointment?  
Yes, appointments can be rescheduled up to 2 hours before the scheduled time.

What is your cancellation policy?  
Cancellations made less than 2 hours before the appointment may incur a cancellation fee.

What happens if I miss my appointment?  
No-shows may result in restrictions on future bookings.

---

### Timing & Arrival

What if I arrive late?  
If you arrive more than 15 minutes late, we may need to shorten or reschedule your appointment.

How long does each service take?  
Service durations vary. For example, a women’s haircut takes about 45 minutes, while balayage may take up to 150 minutes.

Can I combine multiple services in one visit?  
Yes, but please mention this when booking so we can allocate enough time.

---

### Services & Recommendations

Which service is best for dry or damaged hair?  
We recommend a Deep Conditioning Treatment to restore moisture and improve hair health.

What is the difference between balayage and highlights?  
Balayage is a hand-painted technique that creates a natural, blended look, while highlights use foils to create more defined contrast.

Do you offer services for frizzy hair?  
Yes, our Keratin Treatment helps reduce frizz and makes hair smoother and more manageable.

Do you offer children’s haircuts?  
Yes, we provide gentle and efficient haircuts for children under 12.

---
